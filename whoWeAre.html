Who We Are

In the modern world, it seems that any knowledge is attainable. However, in practice, it becomes clear that with each passing period, obtaining true, accurate, and relevant knowledge is becoming increasingly difficult. AIs make mistakes, and a plethora of sources doesn't guarantee the reliability of the information they contain (who actually wrote this article: a doctor with years of experience or a teenage blogger)? Knowing those who know and having the ability to consult them is becoming an asset in a hyper-connected society. In response to these challenges, a group of experts* initiated the formalisation of an open, collaborative community of people interested in mitochondrial health from around the world and the creation of an open knowledge base in collaboration with them.

The key result of Global Mito is the collaboratively created Mito Knowledge Base — a universal navigation map for professionals worldwide. Every session, roundtable, and networking activity is aimed at joint knowledge generation and tangible outcomes.

We bring knowledge management (KM) technologies to Mito, enabling the effective collection, systematization, development, and application of knowledge. Knowledge management experts facilitate the collaboration of the Mito Community participants .

One of the main results of Global MITO is the creation of the base for an intercultural expert community with a global virtual collaborative culture (GVCC). The principles of GVCC allow us to bring together diverse fields and create a compelling multi-stakeholder knowledge base. Each participant becomes a co-author of collective knowledge through exchange, co-creative initiatives, and projects. We integrate the principles of the co-creative culture from the GLINK summits (Global Leaders who Innovate Next Knowledge). It has been bringing together leaders from 30 countries to create innovation since 2017.

Knowledge Management Global Network* (KMGN) – is a not-for profit network of international KM communities and associations that endeavours to connect KM professionals and facilitate co-creation and sharing of KM assets and resources. Founded in 2014.

Vilavi International (global mito-industry business) took the initiative to bring together leading experts in science, practice, and business to systemise knowledge in mitochondrial health and medicine.
