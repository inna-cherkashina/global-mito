What We do

We use science-based knowledge management (KM) techniques and technologies, enabling the effective collection, systematization, development, and application of knowledge. This includes cutting-edge digital tools like GenAI. Experts from KMGN bring best practices into the mito field

We integrate the principles of the co-creative culture from the GLINK summits (Global Leaders who Innovate Next Knowledge) and MXR.world (‘mixers across the world’). Every session, roundtable, and networking activity is aimed at joint knowledge generation with tangible outcomes.

he result of our work will not be just a collection of abstracts, but a complete database architecture - International Mito Knowledge Base: Mito Body of Knowledge and Mito Business ecosystem map.
