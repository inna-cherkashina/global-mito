Knowledge Base

The key result of Global Mito is the collaboratively created Mito Knowledge Base — a universal navigation map for professionals worldwide. It includes Body of Knowledge and Business ecosystem map. The main purpose of co-creation with KMGN is to set up a knowledge process that allows to make knowledge base live, up-to-date, and user-friendly

Mito Body of Knowledge

It systematizes experience and innovations from mito science, practice, and business. Mito Knowledge Base is a complete, organized collection of concepts, theories, data, methods, and best practices that make up the shared understanding of the field. The Body of Knowledge for mitochondrial research, science, and technology is the comprehensive reference framework that organizes

  • what is known (facts, mechanisms, technologies)
  • how it is studied (methods, tools)
  • how it is applied (therapies, diagnostics, innovations).

Mito Business ecosystem map

A business ecosystem map for mitochondrial research, science, and technology is a way to visualize all the interconnected players, institutions, and value flows in the field. Instead of looking at one company or lab in isolation, it shows how the entire landscape works together — from discovery to application.
