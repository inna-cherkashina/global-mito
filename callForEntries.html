GLOBAL MITO 2025–2026 CALL FOR PARTICIPATION NOW OPEN

Applications close May 31, 2026

We are excited to open the international call for participation in Global Mito — global knowledge ecosystem in mitochondrial health.

Now entering its key stage, Global Mito develops in two phases:

2025 — Think Tank (online, Nov 2025 – May 2026): a collaborative program shaping the Global Knowledge Architecture.

2026 — Global Mito Conference (Dubai, Nov 11–12, 2026): a gathering of global leaders to launch the Knowledge Base and showcase applied solutions.

Earlybird Deadline: December 15, 2025 Final Deadline: May 31, 2026

Global Mito is not just a conference. It is an international movement that makes experts visible worldwide and creates navigation across directions, technologies, products, and solutions in mitochondrial health.
