About

Global Mito is a living body of knowledge and a collaborative community powered by Knowledge Management (KM) technologies. Once a year, we gather in person to share best practices, discuss the most influential questions, and network. We built through collaboration and were powered by Knowledge Management (KM) technologies. Originating from the GLINK approach (Global Leaders who Innovate Next Knowledge) since 2017, which has united leaders from 30+ countries, we are creating a shared navigational ecosystem across technologies, products, directions, and experts in the mitochondrial field.

MITO Body
of Knowledge

The heart of Global Mito — a continuously evolving Knowledge Base powered by KM technologies. Access frameworks, research summaries, tech classifications, and innovation maps contributed and refined by the community. The purpose of the knowledge base is to collect and organise diverse, accurate, and up-to-date information on mitochondrial health, valuable for those developing the industry, creating new products and services, and applying this knowledge in their daily practice The structure and procedure for updating the knowledge base were created by a team of KMGN experts, based on the best technologies and knowledge management practices.

MITO collaborative community

A collaborative community bringing together experts and users from various fields (science, medicine, fitness, beauty, wellness, sports, business) interested in improving their quality of life through mitochondrial health management.
